Chorizo Street Tacos

Chorizo street tacos is the perfect meal for beginners to cook for breakfast, lunch, and dinner. It only takes 10 minutes and serves 2 people. This recipe can be easily doubled and extra vegetables, spices, and protein can be added to fit your preferences
Ingredients : - 1 chorizo sausage link, meat crumbled - 2 tablespoons chipotle peppers - 4 corn tortilla - 2 tablespoon chopped onion - 2 tablespoons chopped cilantro (optional)
Method : 1. Combine crumbles chorizo and chipotle peppers in adobo sauce 2. Cook in a skillet until crisp, then set aside 3. Heat tortillas on the skillet 4. Stack two tortillas for each taco and fill with chorizo, onion, and cilantro (add additional veges, spices, or protein to taste) 5. Enjoy!
