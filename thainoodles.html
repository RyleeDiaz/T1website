Thai Noodle

These Thai noodles will add a splash of flavor into your diet. They are so versatile and easy to make when you’re craving noodles. The amount of vegetables thrown into this dish has so many health benefits and boosts the taste of this classic Thai noodle recipe. Serves 3-4 and takes 25 minutes to prep
Ingredients ; - 6 ounces linguini noodles - 1 cup bagged coleslaw mix - ½ cup shredded carrots - ⅓ cup chopped cilantro - ¼ cup chopped green onions - ⅓ cup chopped honey roasted peanuts (optional) - 2 cups shredded rotisserie chicken (optional but recommended) Sauce ingredients: - ¼ cup soy sauce - 2 tablespoons honey - 2 tablespoons sesame oil - 4 cloves minced garlic - ½ teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional, add to taste)
Method : 1. Cook linguine noodle (follow package instructions), then drain 2. Whisk together soy sauce, honey, sesame oil, garlic, and red pepper flakes into a small bowl for the sauce 3. Pour the sauce over the drained noodles and toss to combine 4. Add shredded cabbage, carrots, and cilantro to the noodle mixture and mix 5. Gently stir in half of the chopped cilantro, green onions, peanuts (optional), and shredded chicken (optional). Reserve the remaining for garnish 6. Serve warm or cold, garnished with remaining cilantro, green onions, and chopped peanuts 7. Enjoy!
