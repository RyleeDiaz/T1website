Pizza Slider

Kid friendly, fun mini sliders that taste just like pizza! This dish is healthy and delicious for the whole family to enjoy. Our pizza sliders are also an easy party meal that everybody will love. Serves 4 and takes 25 minutes to prepare.

Ingredients:
- 1 pound Lean ground beef
- 1 tablespoon chopped parsley
- 2 teaspoon chopped oregano
- ½ teaspoon ground pepper
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ⅓ cup marinara sauce
- 4 ounces fresh mozzarella
- 8 small leaves Boston lettuce
- 8 whole wheat dinner rolls, toasted

Method:
1. Preheat grill (or pan) to medium high heat
2. Mix beef, parsley, oregano, pepper, and salt in a bowl until combines
3. Divide the seasoned beef mixture into 8 portions and shape into 1 inch thick patties (ensure they are relatively even in size and slightly larger than the buns)
4. Grill the patties until they are medium rare, about 2-4 minutes per side
5. Spoon marinara sauce onto each patty and top with mozzarella cheese
6. Grill until the cheese melts (about 2 minutes) and add lettuce to the slider
7. Enjoy!
