One Pot Chicken and Vegetable Skillet

This one pot chicken and vegetables recipe only takes 20 minutes to cook and will satisfy the whole family. This is a super easy recipe that is healthy and nutritious for everybody
Ingredients: - 1lb (450g) skinless, boneless chicken breasts (cut into 1 inch pieces) - 2 sliced zucchini - 2 red bell peppers (chopped in chunks) - 1 broccoli crown (broken into florets) - ½ onion, sliced - ¼ teaspoon salt and pepper - 2 tablespoons olive oil - 2 tablespoons cajun seasoning - ¼ cup chicken broth - 1 tablespoon freshly cut parsley - ½ teaspoon red chili pepper flakes (optional)
Method : 1. Season chicken with salt, pepper, olive oil, and cajun seasoning in a bowl 2. Cook chicken over medium heat on a skillet, and set aside 3. Stir fry the onions, broccoli, zucchini, and bell pepper. Season this with cajun seasoning until vegetables are tender 4. Deglaze the skillet with chicken stock then add the cooked chicken back on and mix vegetables and chicken together 5. Garnish with parsley and chili pepper flakes (optional) 6. Enjoy!
