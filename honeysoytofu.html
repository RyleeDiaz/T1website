    • Honey and Soy Tofu Stir Fry

    This easy and delicious meal satisfies 4 mouths and takes minimal time to cook (15 minutes). The tofu can easily be substituted for chicken or whatever protein is preferred in your household. Our Honey and soy tofu stir fry recipe is a healthy, nutrient filled meal while still providing for your cravings.

    Ingredients:
    - 2 cups of rice
    - 400g tofu (protein substitute)
    - 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
    - 450g of boiled broccoli
    - 200g of chopped carrots
    - 200g of corn
    - 40g cut fresh garlic
    - 100g honey
    - 100g soy sauce
    - Handful of sesame seeds

    Optionals :
    - Â½ cup of chopped/roasted cashews

    Method :
    1. Cook rice (packet or rice cooker)
    2. Cut tofu (or meat substitute) into long thick strips
    3. Heat oil over high heat and cook tofu until light golden brown. (or cook meat)
    4. Cut carrots into small cubes and cut broccoli into pieces
    5. Add broccoli and carrots to the pan and stir with tofu until heated, add corn
    6. Make honey soy sauce by mixing garlic, honey, soy sauce, and sesame seeds
    7. Present dish by adding veggies and tofu on top of rice then drizzle the honey soy sauce on top (add crushed cashews on top - optional)
