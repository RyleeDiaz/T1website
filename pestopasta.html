Pesto Spinach Pasta

Our family-favorite pesto spinach pasta meal is an easy, healthy meal that is also vegetarian (although you could add your preference of meat for protein). This is a kids favorite and is suitable for busy parents wanting to quickly cook up a yummy meal. The Pesto Spinach Pasta serves 4 people.
Ingredients : - 400g wholemeal penne - 5 tbsp vegetarian basil pesto - 500g roughly chopped baby spinach - 220g cherry tomatoes - parmesan
Method : 1. Boil a large pot of water with salt 2. Add penne pasta and cook for the length of time of the packet 3. Before draining, scoop out 1 cup of the pasta cooking water 4. Drain pasta and leave for a minute 5. Transfer pasta to a bowl and mix in vegetarian basil pesto and Â¼ cup of the pasta cooking water (add preferred amount of water to make pasta more silky) 6. Top with chopped baby spinach, cherry tomatoes, and parmesan
